# Locd'by Roe — Official Website

Welcome to the official website for **Locd'by Roe**, a Kansas City-based hair salon specializing in locs, braids, natural hair styling, and barber services.

## 👩🏾‍🎨 About Aurora "Roe"

Licensed cosmetologist since 2010, Aurora — known as Roe — offers a full range of hair services from starter locs and loc maintenance to box braids, fades, and Sisterlocks™ consultations. Located in Kansas City, MO.
